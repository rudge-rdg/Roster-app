Legend (Color Guide)
Orange Border: Player is currently ON COURT.
Blue Border: "Pending" sub (Selected to swap).
Navy Bar: Section for on court players.
Yellow Bar: Section for available bench.
Label Meanings
• ROT: Number of rotations (shifts) played.
• Sat: Minutes sitting on the bench during the current rest period.
• Shift: Time remaining until the next planned substitution.
• Total: Overall game clock (Total running time).
1. Check-In Toggle off missing players in the CHECK-IN tab. Only "On" players will show up in the game rotation.
2. Start Game Select 5 starters (they turn blue) and hit "START GAME." The game and shift timers begin immediately.
3. Subs Tap an active player to sub out and a bench player to sub in. Once exactly 5 are set, press "SET ROSTER." The shift timer resets.
4. Fair Play Sorting The bench automatically puts players with the least playing time and longest rest at the top.
